FAQ

Common pricing questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and retain access until the end of your billing period.
Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept payments via Paystack (card, USSD, bank transfer), Stripe (global card payments in USD), and PayPal.
What currency are prices in?
All prices are in US Dollars ($). When you checkout with Paystack, the equivalent amount is charged in your local currency at the current exchange rate.
What happens after I subscribe?
After payment, your service is activated and a setup wizard guides you through configuration. Your analytics dashboard becomes live once setup is complete.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a free 30-minute discovery call so you can understand exactly what you'll get before subscribing. Our consultants will walk you through everything.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or add more services at any time. Contact us and we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
