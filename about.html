GTS Amplify is the flagship platform of Guided Tech Solutions — helping Nigerian and African businesses grow smarter with data-driven technology services.

Our Mission

Making enterprise technology accessible to every business

GTS Amplify was born from a simple observation: the technology tools that large corporations use to grow — lead generation systems, cloud infrastructure, marketing automation, SEO analytics — were either too expensive or too complex for small and medium businesses in Nigeria.

We built GTS Amplify to change that. By combining expert execution with a transparent, real-time dashboard, every business can now access enterprise-grade growth services and see exactly what they're getting for their investment.
