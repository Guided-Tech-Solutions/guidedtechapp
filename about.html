🏢 About GTS Amplify

deliver great results

GTS Amplify is powered by Guided Tech Solutions — a Mississippi-based technology company empowering businesses, schools, and municipalities with innovative, dependable technology solutions.

What We Are

We help global brands deliver great results

We simplify complex technology challenges, improve operational efficiency, and help organizations grow through innovative, dependable solutions. Our reliable systems empower clients to confidently navigate an evolving technology landscape.

Our mission is to modernize Mississippi's critical infrastructure one city, one school, and one business at a time. We deliver secure, compliant, and resilient technology with zero downtime and zero overwhelm, ensuring readiness for CJIS, NIST, and CMMC standards.

We envision a Mississippi where every organization — from small businesses to municipal governments — has access to enterprise-grade technology that is secure, efficient, and built for long-term growth.

Our goal is to be the most trusted technology partner in the region, known for delivering results that last and relationships that endure.

Guided Tech Solutions began with a mission to transform the way organizations adapt to modern technology. Our focus has always been on empowering communities, teams, and leaders with tools that promote efficiency, security, and sustainable growth.

Over the years, our experience and passion have allowed us to deliver impactful solutions for municipalities, schools, and small businesses. We continue to evolve, innovate, and guide our partners toward a stronger digital future.
